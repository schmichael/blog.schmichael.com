Frustrated with IMAP

  2007-04-24

I’ve been googling to try and find information on implementing an IMAP client (especially in Python). I ran across a post to the Twisted-Python mailing list which pretty well sums up why there aren’t any good* IMAP clients in existence.

Don’t expect [CherryMail][2]** to save you anytime soon…

* Sure Thunderbird and Evolution handle IMAP alright, but you still have to handle them with care.

** My CherryMail post has already made it to the 2nd search result returned for “uglier than squirrelmail“… excellent…***
