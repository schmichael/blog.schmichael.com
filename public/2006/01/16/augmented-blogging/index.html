Augmented Blogging

  2006-01-17

Aaron was kind enough to upgrade my blog to WordPress 2.0 along with his own. I’m going to try and reorganize my blog as the organic method of creating categories as they occur to me has worked out suboptimally, and the default theme is a bit boring.
