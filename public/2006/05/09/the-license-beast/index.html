The License Beast

  2006-05-09

An article over at Computer World is indirectly one of the best arguments for Free Software I’ve ever read. I’ve attempted to navigate [Microsoft’s various licensing schemes][3] for small businesses before, and its always been a confusing and ardous process.

The whole idea of per-seat software licensing seems inherently flawed to me. A great deal of time and resources seems to be wasted on it by both vendors and consumers.
