SF.net Stability Issues

  2005-08-04

Looks like I’d better hurry and get OpenIT 2.0-RC2 out before August 9th.

I have to admit, I’m a bit frustrated with SourceForge.net. I appreciate the service they provide, but it seems like the number of ads keeps increasing while the level of service stays the same (at best!). To make matters worse I can’t get SSH keys to work with developer CVS access. My keys work when logging into shell.sf.net, but I’m still prompted for my password when using CVS.

In the office we’ve switched nearly all of our projects from CVS to [Subversion][3], and we couldn’t be happier.

[3]: http://subversion.tigris.org/
