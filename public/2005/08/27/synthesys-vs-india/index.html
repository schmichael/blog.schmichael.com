Synthesys vs. India

  2005-08-27

Looks like Synthesys is part of a larger movement where IT jobs are spring up in rural America. For anyone who happens across this blog that isn’t familiar with Synthesys, we’re located in Greenville, IL, which is about 45 minutes from St. Louis, MO.

We’ve been (happily) shocked at the amount of IT work we’ve found in a town of 8,000. While HPUGOA is an out of town customer, most of our other business is local. We’ve built two custom commercial applications for local organizations. One is [Bid-Advantage][5], and the other is just about to be released to the public.

Living in Greenville definitely helps to keep the costs down, but has one major disadvantage: what is my Art Major wife supposed to do?

[5]: http://www.bid-advantage.net/
