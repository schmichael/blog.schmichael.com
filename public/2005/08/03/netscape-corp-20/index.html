Netscape Corp. 2.0

  2005-08-03

A Mozilla Corporation has been founded (nice article about it on mozillaZine). Please tell me I’m not the only one whose first thought was “Wait… isn’t this just Netscape Corp. 2.0?” Hopefully Mozilla Corp. fairs better than its ancestors.

Update: [Asa][4] clarifies that because of the subsidiary status of Moz Corp, there won’t be any IPOs or other Netscape-esque excitement.
