Breaking the Law

  2005-11-08

I’m such a rebel.

If you don’t want to read my long winded rambling posts, let me summarize:

I want to use icons from the CC-SA licensed Tango project in OpenIT which uses the GPL. Some people say they’re incompatible, and now [Jakub Steiner][6] has the Novell legal team looking into it!

I’m about to commit the new icons to OpenIT’s CVS with could possibly forever incriminate OpenIT and destroy both the free software and free culture movements at the same time.

Or maybe no one will notice and/or care… …which is the far more likely outcome.

[6]: http://jimmac.musichall.cz/
